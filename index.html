Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

1. Introduction

AgroZona ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our WhatsApp Business services.

2. Information We Collect

When you communicate with us through WhatsApp Business API, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Your phone number and WhatsApp profile name
  • Message Content: Messages you send to us regarding orders, inquiries, and support
  • Order Information: Product details, quantities, delivery addresses, and payment preferences
  • Technical Data: Message timestamps and delivery status information
  • Usage Data: Interaction patterns and communication preferences

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Processing and fulfilling your agricultural product orders
  • Sending order confirmations, updates, and delivery notifications
  • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our products and services
  • Complying with legal obligations and business requirements

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent for WhatsApp communications
  • Contract Performance: Processing is necessary to fulfill your orders
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing customer support and improving services

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted logistics partners for order delivery (limited to delivery-related data only)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business Protection: To protect our rights, property, or safety

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure data storage and backup systems

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Order Data: 3 years from the last order for accounting and warranty purposes
  • Communication Records: 2 years for customer service quality and dispute resolution
  • Marketing Consent: Until you withdraw consent or request deletion

8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing purposes
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for WhatsApp communications at any time

9. Opt-Out and Communication Preferences

You can opt-out of WhatsApp communications at any time by:

  • Sending "STOP" or "UNSUBSCRIBE" via WhatsApp
  • Contacting our support team directly
  • Emailing us at the contact information below

Please note that you may continue to receive transactional messages related to ongoing orders even after opting out of marketing communications.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers, including adequacy decisions or standard contractual clauses approved by relevant authorities.

11. Cookies and Tracking

Our WhatsApp Business integration does not use cookies. However, our website may use cookies for functionality and analytics. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for detailed information.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We will notify you of any material changes through WhatsApp or email, and the updated policy will be posted on our website with a new "Last Updated" date.
